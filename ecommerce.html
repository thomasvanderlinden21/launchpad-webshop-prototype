Frequently asked questions

What is an online payment gateway?
An online payment gateway is a technology that securely captures and transmits payment data between a customer, merchant, and bank. It authorises card transactions in real time, handles encryption, and ensures funds are settled correctly into your account.
Which payment methods are supported?
Worldline supports 150+ payment methods including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, iDEAL, Bancontact, SOFORT, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay+, WeChat Pay, and many more local methods across 40+ markets.
How does Worldline protect against fraud?
Our AI-powered fraud detection analyses every transaction in real time using machine learning models trained on billions of data points. Combined with 3DS2 authentication and a smart exemption engine, we reduce fraud while keeping friction low for genuine customers.
How long does integration take?
Most merchants go live within days using our hosted payment pages or pre-built plugins for Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and other major platforms. For custom API integrations, our developer documentation and dedicated technical support team will guide you through every step.
