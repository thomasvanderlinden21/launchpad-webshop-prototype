FAQ

Common questions

What is blended pricing?

Blended pricing rolls interchange, acquirer, and card scheme fees into a single transparent rate. You see one number on every transaction — no itemised breakdown of what Visa, your bank, or Worldline each charged. Simple to budget, simple to reconcile.

Are there any setup or monthly fees?

On the Starter plan: no monthly fees, no activation fees, no minimum volume. You only pay per transaction processed. The Growth plan (€19/month) unlocks lower per-transaction rates — it typically covers itself at around €700/month in card turnover.

When do I receive payouts?

By default, funds settle to your bank account the next business day (T+1). You can switch to weekly or monthly payouts from your dashboard — there's no extra charge for any schedule.

Is there a contract or lock-in period?

No. Starter and Growth are month-to-month — cancel at any time with no penalty. There are no minimum turnover requirements and no cancellation fees. Hardware purchases are final but independent of your service agreement.

Can I accept multiple currencies?

Yes — Worldline supports 12 currencies. Non-euro transactions are converted at the daily interbank rate plus a 1% FX fee. Payouts are settled in euros by default; multi-currency settlement accounts are available for Enterprise merchants.

What's included in the Enterprise plan?

Enterprise is a fully custom engagement. You get negotiated interchange-plus (IC+) rates, a dedicated account manager, custom SLAs, ERP/POS integrations, and country-specific acquiring optimisation. Pricing is agreed during onboarding based on your volume and mix.
